Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our EFI products and Hackintosh builds.

A Hackintosh is a computer that runs macOS on hardware not officially supported by Apple. Our EFI solutions make it possible to run macOS on compatible PC hardware.

Use our compatibility checker to find out if your hardware is compatible. Generally, Intel CPUs (8th gen and newer) and AMD Ryzen CPUs with compatible motherboards and AMD graphics cards work best.

Contact us for a custom compatibility assessment. We can create custom EFI solutions for hardware configurations not listed in our standard offerings.

Our EFI packages include a complete EFI folder with all necessary kexts, config.plist, and drivers for your hardware. We also provide detailed installation instructions and post-install optimization guides.

We offer refunds if our EFI solution doesn't work with the hardware you specified during purchase. Note that purchases made for incompatible hardware (not verified through our compatibility checker) are non-refundable.
